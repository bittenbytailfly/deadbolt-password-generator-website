Privacy Policy

Deadbolt Password Generator does not collect any personal information, or transmit any password information that could identify you as an individual as this is against the entire ethos of what Deadbolt is about. However, as with most websites there are some things you should be aware of.

Web Site

The website uses Google Analytics, which tracks information such as:

  • The website you came from to get here
  • How long you stay here
  • What kind of computer you are using
  • Whether or not you generated a password

No information about your password is ever transmitted or stored. For further information regarding Google Analytics please see their Terms of Service.

Google AdSense is used for advertising purposes (Deadbolt is free remember, but hosting is not) - for more information about Google's advertising and to opt out please see their Privacy & Terms.

Mobile App

As with the website, no information is ever transmitted or stored on Deadbolt servers, however in order to function there are some permissions that are necessary:

  • INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: Deadbolt is based on the Crosswalk Browser - a webview for enhanced compatibility that requires these permissions.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Profiles are stored in your device's memory.
  • CAMERA, FLASHLIGHT: Profiles can be imported from the Chrome extension - this is achieved by scanning a QR code which requires the use of the camera and flash.
